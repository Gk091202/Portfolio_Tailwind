How I Designed My Portfolio

Published on June 21, 2025 • by Gaurav Koli

Designing a portfolio website was both a technical and creative challenge. I wanted it to reflect not just my skills, but also my personality.

I started by laying out a simple structure using HTML and Tailwind CSS. I made sure the layout was mobile-friendly and easy to navigate.

The color palette was chosen to evoke trust and clarity. I used animations sparingly to keep it modern but not overwhelming.

If you're looking to build your own, I highly recommend starting with a wireframe and planning your content carefully.
